## Seasonal Tips

- **Mar–May**: Best weather; Semana Santa processions (Seville is spectacular)
- **Jun–Aug**: Hot (Madrid/Seville can exceed 40°C), crowded, peak prices
- **Sep–Oct**: Ideal — warm, fewer crowds, Feria events
- **Nov–Feb**: Cheap, uncrowded; cold in Madrid/Segovia; mild on coasts

## Transport (AVE High-Speed Rail)

- Madrid ↔ Barcelona: ~2.5 hours
- Madrid ↔ Toledo: 30 minutes (from Atocha)
- Madrid ↔ Segovia: 30 minutes (from Chamartín)
- Madrid ↔ Seville: ~2.5 hours
- Madrid ↔ Granada: ~3.5 hours (some routes change at Antequera)
- Barcelona ↔ Seville: ~5.5 hours
