Madrid is Spain's capital and largest city, set on the central Castilian plateau at 650m altitude. It combines world-class art museums (the "Golden Triangle of Art"), a magnificent royal palace, vibrant tapas culture, and lively nightlife. Population approximately 3.3 million.

## Top Sights

### Museo del Prado
Spain's greatest art museum; one of the world's finest collections.
- Adult €15, under 18 free
- **Free**: Mon–Sat 6–8pm, Sun 5–7pm (queue 30–45 min before)
- Paseo del Arte combo (Prado + Reina Sofía + Thyssen): €31
- Highlights: Velázquez's *Las Meninas*, Goya's *Pinturas Negras*, El Greco, Rubens, Titian

### Museo Reina Sofía
20th-century Spanish art; home to Picasso's *Guernica*.
- Adult €10/free; free Mon (except Tue), Wed–Sat 7–9pm, Sun after 1:30pm
- Also: Dalí, Miró, Juan Gris

### Museo Thyssen-Bornemisza
Bridges the gap between Prado and Reina Sofía; broad European art history.
- Adult €13/free

### Palacio Real (Royal Palace)
The world's largest royal palace by floor area; still used for state ceremonies.
- Adult €12/free; some free access for EU/EEA citizens on Mon and Wed
- Hours: 10am–8pm (Apr–Sep), 10am–6pm (Oct–Mar)
- Highlights: Armería Real (Royal Armory), state rooms, Farmacia Real

### Parque del Retiro
Madrid's beloved 140-hectare central park; gardens, ponds, free galleries.
- Free entry; rowing boats on the lake €6/hr
- Palacio de Cristal inside: free temporary exhibitions

### Plaza Mayor
Historic 17th-century plaza; former site of bullfights and Inquisition trials.
- Free; tourist information office on-site
- Surrounding bars are touristy; better to browse than eat here

## Paseo del Arte (Art Walk)

Three great museums along Paseo del Prado — the Paseo del Arte combo ticket (€31) covers all three:
1. **Prado** (north)
2. **Thyssen-Bornemisza** (northwest)
3. **Reina Sofía** (south, near Atocha station)

## Getting There & Around

- **Fly**: Adolfo Suárez Madrid-Barajas Airport (MAD); direct from Korea (Korean Air, Iberia)
- **Metro**: Line 8 from airport to Nuevos Ministerios (€4.50–5 airport supplement)
- **City transport**: Metrobús 10-trip card ~€12.20
- **Day trips**: Toledo 30 min AVE (Atocha), Segovia 30 min Avant (Chamartín)

## Practical Tips

- **Best time**: Spring (Mar–May) and autumn (Sep–Oct); summer can exceed 40°C
- Free museum evenings are crowded; arrive 30–45 min before the free window opens
- Madrid eats and drinks late: lunch 2–3pm, dinner 9–10pm, bars open until 3am+
- El Rastro flea market: Sunday mornings in La Latina (free to browse)
- Caixaforum (beside Prado): excellent temporary exhibitions at low cost €6–8

## Food Highlights

- **Tapas staples**: patatas bravas, jamón ibérico, tortilla española, gambas al ajillo
- *Bocadillo de calamares* (squid sandwich): Madrid institution near Plaza Mayor
- *Churros con chocolate*: breakfast staple; Chocolatería San Ginés (open 24h)
- *Cocido madrileño*: hearty chickpea stew; winter specialty
